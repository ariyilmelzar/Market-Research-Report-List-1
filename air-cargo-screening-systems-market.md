<p><ul><li>Explosive Detection Systems (EDS)</li><li>Explosive Trace Detection (ETD)</li><li>Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air cargo screening systems market comprises several key technologies. Explosive Detection Systems (EDS) utilize advanced scanning to identify explosives within cargo by analyzing the contents' density and chemical signatures. Explosive Trace Detection (ETD) employs swabbing techniques to detect trace amounts of explosives, ensuring heightened security. Non-computed Tomography Transmission X-ray (Non-CT X-ray) systems provide two-dimensional imaging of cargo, allowing for quick assessment of package contents. Together, these technologies enhance safety and compliance in air cargo operations.</p></p>
