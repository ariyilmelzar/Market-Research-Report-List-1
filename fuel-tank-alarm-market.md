<p><strong>The Fuel Tank Alarm Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ultrasonic Type</li><li>Float Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fuel tank alarm market is segmented into ultrasonic and float type alarms. Ultrasonic type alarms utilize sound waves to detect fuel levels, providing accurate data without physical contact with the liquid. They are favored for their reliability and minimal maintenance needs. In contrast, float type alarms operate through physical float mechanisms that rise and fall with the fuel level, triggering alarms when thresholds are reached. Each type caters to specific applications, balancing cost, technology, and user requirements in fuel monitoring.</p></p>
