<p><strong>The Variable Air Volume Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Duct VAV</li><li>Dual Duct VAV</li><li>Induction VAV</li><li>Fan Powered VAV</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Variable Air Volume (VAV) systems are categorized into several types: </p><p>1. **Single Duct VAV** systems deliver conditioned air through a single duct where airflow varies to meet demand, providing efficient temperature control.</p><p>2. **Dual Duct VAV** systems mix hot and cold air in two separate ducts to achieve desired temperature, enhancing comfort in diverse spaces.</p><p>3. **Induction VAV** systems utilize a combination of primary airflow and secondary air, promoting energy efficiency.</p><p>4. **Fan Powered VAV** units include a fan for supplementary heating or cooling, improving performance in varying load conditions.</p></p>
