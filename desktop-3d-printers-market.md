<p><strong>The Desktop 3D Printers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Material Extrusion Technology</li><li>Light Polymerization Technology</li><li>Other Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The desktop 3D printer market is primarily categorized into three types based on technology. Material Extrusion Technology, commonly known as FDM, involves melting thermoplastic filaments layer by layer to create objects. Light Polymerization Technology, including SLA and DLP, uses light to cure liquid resin into solid forms, producing high-detail prints. Other technologies encompass methods like binder jetting and powder bed fusion, offering diverse materials and applications. Each type serves various user needs in prototyping, hobbyist projects, and small-scale production.</p></p>
