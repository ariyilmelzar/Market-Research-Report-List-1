<p><p>Equivalent time sampling oscilloscopes (ETSO) are specialized measurement tools that capture repetitive signals with high precision by sampling intervals that exceed the signal period. This technique enables the observation of signals that are too fast for conventional real-time oscilloscopes. ETSOs are essential in applications like telecommunications, radar systems, and high-speed digital design, where analyzing fast phenomena is critical. </p><p>The Equivalent Time Sampling Oscilloscopes Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 7.4% during the forecast period, driven by increasing demand for high-frequency signal analysis in various industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and aerospace.
