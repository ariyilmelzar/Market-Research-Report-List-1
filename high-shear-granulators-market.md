<p><p>The High Shear Granulators market is segmented into three primary types based on the granulation medium: dry powders, aqueous, and solvent granulations. Dry powder granulation involves the compaction of powder materials to enhance flow and dissolution properties without the use of liquids. Aqueous granulation utilizes water as a binder, promoting uniform dispersion and improving product consistency. Solvent granulation incorporates organic solvents, enabling specific property enhancements in granulated products while carefully managing volatility and safety considerations.</p></p>
