<p><p>The lithium jump starters market features two main types: self-contained units and plug-in units. Self-contained jump starters are portable devices with built-in batteries, allowing users to jump-start vehicles without needing an external power source. These units are compact and convenient for emergency situations. Plug-in units, on the other hand, require a connection to an external power source, such as a household outlet, making them typically more powerful but less portable. Both types cater to different consumer needs for vehicle jump-starting solutions.</p></p>
