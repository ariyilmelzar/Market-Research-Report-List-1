<p><p>The automotive particulate matter sensor market is categorized based on the size of particulate matter measured. PM1.0 sensors detect particles with a diameter of 1 micron or less, crucial for understanding respiratory health impacts. PM2.5 sensors measure particles up to 2.5 microns, often associated with combustion processes. PM10 sensors capture particles up to 10 microns, which can include dust and pollen. Other sensors may focus on specific applications or broader ranges, addressing various environmental and regulatory needs in the automotive sector.</p></p>
