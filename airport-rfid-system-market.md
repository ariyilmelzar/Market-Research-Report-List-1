<p><strong>The Airport RFID System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Frequency</li><li>High Frequency</li><li>Ultra-High Frequency</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airport RFID System Market is categorized into three frequency types: Low Frequency (LF), High Frequency (HF), and Ultra-High Frequency (UHF). LF (125-134 kHz) is used for close-range applications, such as baggage tracking, due to its resistance to interference. HF (13.56 MHz) offers moderate read distances and is often employed for ticketing and access control. UHF (860-960 MHz) provides longer read ranges and faster tag read rates, making it ideal for real-time tracking of passengers and luggage in airports.</p></p>
