<p><p>The flexible printed circuits (FPC) market comprises three main types: single panel, double panel, and multilayer panel. Single panels consist of one conductive layer on a flexible substrate, suitable for simple applications. Double panels feature two conductive layers, allowing for more complex circuitry while still maintaining flexibility. Multilayer panels incorporate multiple conductive layers stacked together, providing advanced functionalities and compact designs for intricate devices. These types cater to various industries, including consumer electronics, automotive, and medical applications, enhancing device performance and design flexibility.</p></p>
