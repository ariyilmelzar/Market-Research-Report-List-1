<p><ul><li>Liquid-Liquid Chromatography (LLC)</li><li>Liquid-Solid Chromatography (LSC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Chromatography Systems market comprises various types, including Liquid-Liquid Chromatography (LLC) and Liquid-Solid Chromatography (LSC). LLC separates components based on differences in their solubilities in liquid phases, making it suitable for analyzing compounds in diverse solvents. In contrast, LSC utilizes a solid stationary phase and a liquid mobile phase, focusing on interactions between the liquid sample and solid material for effective separation. Both methods are vital in pharmaceuticals, environmental testing, and food safety applications.</p></p>
