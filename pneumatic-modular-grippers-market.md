<p><p>The pneumatic modular grippers market primarily consists of two types: parallel grippers and angular grippers. Parallel grippers operate by opening and closing with fingers that move parallel to each other, making them ideal for gripping flat or symmetrical objects. In contrast, angular grippers feature fingers that move in an angular motion, allowing them to grasp irregularly shaped items effectively. Both types are utilized in automation and robotics, enhancing operational efficiency and precision in various industrial applications.</p></p>
