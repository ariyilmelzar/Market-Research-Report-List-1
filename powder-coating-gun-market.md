<p><ul><li>Corona Powder Coating Gun</li><li>Tribo Powder Coating Gun</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The powder coating gun market features two primary types: Corona and Tribo powder coating guns. </p><p>Corona powder coating guns operate by using high-voltage electrical charges to attract charged powder particles to the grounded substrate, ensuring even coverage. In contrast, Tribo powder coating guns rely on the friction between the powder particles and the gun barrel, allowing for effective charging without high voltages. Both technologies cater to diverse application needs, with variations in efficiency, finish quality, and suitability for different materials and environments.</p></p>
