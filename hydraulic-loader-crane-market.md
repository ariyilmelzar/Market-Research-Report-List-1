<p><p>The hydraulic loader crane market features two main types: knuckle boom cranes and telescopic cranes. Knuckle boom cranes are characterized by their articulated arms, allowing for greater maneuverability and the ability to access tight spaces, making them ideal for urban environments. Conversely, telescopic cranes utilize extendable booms that provide a longer reach, suitable for lifting heavy loads over greater distances, often used in construction and industrial applications. Both types enhance efficiency in material handling tasks.</p></p>
