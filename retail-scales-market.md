<p><p>Retail scales are categorized into two main market types: dual-display and single-display. Single-display scales feature one interface that shows weight and price to the retailer, primarily serving individual transactions. In contrast, dual-display scales have two screens, allowing both the customer and the retailer to view the information simultaneously, enhancing transparency and communication during transactions. Each type serves specific retail needs, with dual-display scales often preferred in busy environments for improved customer interaction and satisfaction.</p></p>
