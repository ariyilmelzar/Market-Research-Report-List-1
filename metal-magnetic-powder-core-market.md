<p><p>The Metal Magnetic Powder Core market comprises various types: MPP Core, Sendust Core, High Flux, and Fe-Si. MPP Cores are known for low core losses and high permeability, making them suitable for high-frequency applications. Sendust Cores utilize a silicon-aluminum alloy, offering good performance in audio frequencies. High Flux Cores provide exceptional magnetic saturation, ideal for power transformers. Fe-Si is a silicon-iron alloy core, known for its cost-effectiveness and efficiency in energy applications. Each core type serves specific industrial requirements.</p></p>
