<p><p>The Air Bag Opening Valve market is primarily categorized into two types: Continuous Airbag Opening Valves and Metering Bag Opening Valves. Continuous Airbag Opening Valves are designed for uninterrupted inflation, ensuring rapid deployment during a collision. In contrast, Metering Bag Opening Valves regulate the flow of gas into the airbag, allowing for controlled inflation based on impact severity. Both types enhance passenger safety by optimizing airbag performance, adapting to varying crash conditions while ensuring timely deployment to mitigate injuries.</p></p>
